What's New

  • IBDP Programme

    Adapting from the famous saying ascribed to an unidentified “wit” by Albert Einstien, “Education is that which remains, after the content is forgotten.” Often in the world of education the emphasis is on learning content rather than learning ‘how to learn’. However the challenges of the changing world demand us to focus on equipping students with the abilities to learn independently. We aim to inculcate in our students the skills to build, to resolve, to empathize and to evolve. We welcome your child to embrace the challenges of the diploma programme founded on a tradition that has groomed leaders for more than a century.
